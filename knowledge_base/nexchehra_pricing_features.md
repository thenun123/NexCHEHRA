# NexCHEHRA Pricing & Features Guide

## Pricing Overview
NexCHEHRA operates on a per-generation pricing model. Costs depend on the video duration and audio mode selected.

### Per-Second Cost Breakdown

| Audio Mode | Cost Per Second | 15-Second Video Cost | Description |
|-----------|----------------|---------------------|-------------|
| **Audio Off** | $0.112/s | ~$1.68 | Silent video, no voice |
| **Audio On** | $0.168/s | ~$2.52 | AI-generated voice speaking your script |
| **Voice Control** | $0.196/s | ~$2.94 | Custom voice cloning (Pro feature) |

### What's Included in Each Generation
- Phase 0 (NexBrain): AI script and prompt generation — **free** (included)
- Phase 1 (NexVision): HD portrait generation — **included in video cost**
- Phase 2 (NexMotion): Video animation with lip-sync — **charged per second**

### Cost Estimation
The dashboard shows a real-time cost estimate before you generate. The estimate updates as you edit your script length or change audio settings. You always know the cost before committing to generation.

## Product Placement Feature
NexCHEHRA includes a powerful product placement system for creating brand advertisement videos.

### How It Works
1. Toggle ON **"Add Product Placement"** on the dashboard.
2. Upload a product image (JPG, PNG, or WebP).
3. Enter the **product name** and optionally **key features** (e.g., "vitamin C, organic, lightweight").
4. Choose a **placement style**:

| Placement | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **In Hand** | Influencer holds the product naturally |
| **Beside** | Product placed right beside the influencer |
| **On Table** | Product displayed on a table in front |
| **Background** | Product visible in the background |
| **Wearing** | Influencer wearing or using the product |

5. The AI will:
   - Integrate the product into the generated image (via Flux Kontext Multi)
   - Write a script mentioning the product's features using the Hook-Demo-Close formula
   - Animate the influencer naturally interacting with the product

### Tips for Great Product Ads
- Use clear, well-lit product photos with transparent or clean backgrounds.
- Provide specific features so the AI can mention them in the script.
- "In Hand" and "On Table" placements tend to look most natural.
- The AI follows a proven ad structure: Hook (grab attention) → Demo (show benefits) → Close (call to action).

## Voice Cloning Feature
NexCHEHRA supports voice cloning for users who want their AI influencer to speak in a specific voice.

### How to Use Voice Cloning
1. Record or prepare a clean 30-second audio clip of the voice you want to clone.
2. Ensure the audio has minimal background noise and clear speech.
3. Upload the clip in the Voice settings when generating a video.
4. The cloned voice will be used for all future videos until changed.

### Voice Cloning Rules
- You may only clone voices that you own or have explicit permission to use.
- Cloning celebrity or public figure voices without consent violates the Acceptable Use Policy.
- Voice cloning is a Pro-tier feature.

## Custom Reference Image
Instead of selecting from the pre-built influencer gallery, you can upload your own reference image.

### Supported Formats
- JPG, JPEG, PNG, WebP
- Maximum file size: 16 MB
- Best results with: clear, front-facing photo, good lighting, neutral or simple background

### How It Works
1. On the dashboard, click the **"+ Custom"** card in the influencer gallery.
2. Upload your reference image.
3. The AI preserves the facial identity from your uploaded photo.
4. Describe the desired outfit, setting, and lighting in the scene description.
5. The generated portrait will look like your uploaded person but in the new setting.

### Tips for Best Results
- Use photos where the face is clearly visible and well-lit.
- Avoid photos with heavy filters, sunglasses, or face-covering accessories.
- Front-facing photos work better than angled shots.
- Higher resolution images produce better results.
