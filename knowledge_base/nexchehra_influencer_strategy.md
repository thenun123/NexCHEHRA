## Recommended Niche Strategy (One Character = One Niche)

Based on 2026 trending niches and character profiles:

### 🌟 **AIRA → Beauty & Skincare Niche**

**Why This Works:**
- Elegant, traditional aesthetic = premium beauty positioning
- Female audience dominates beauty content (80%+ engagement)
- Beauty/skincare is #1 trending product category in 2026

**Trending Products for Aira:**
1. **Peptide Serums** - Anti-aging, science-backed skincare
2. **Vitamin C Products** - Brightening, glow-focused
3. **K-Beauty Products** - Korean skincare expanding globally
4. **Natural/Herbal Skincare** - Matches her traditional aesthetic
5. **Luxury Face Oils** - Premium positioning
6. **Sheet Masks** - Quick results, visual appeal
7. **Hyaluronic Acid Products** - Hydration trend
8. **SPF Moisturizers** - Daily essential

**Content Style:**
- "Glowing skin secrets"
- "My morning skincare routine"
- "This serum changed my skin"
- Traditional elegance + modern beauty science

**Target Audience:** Women 25-45, beauty enthusiasts, premium buyers
