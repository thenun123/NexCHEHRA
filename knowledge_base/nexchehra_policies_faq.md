# NexCHEHRA Platform Policies & FAQ

## 1. General Overview
**What is NexCHEHRA?**
NexCHEHRA is an advanced AI-powered platform designed for generating virtual influencers and digital personas. The platform operates on a three-phase AI generation pipeline:
- **NexBrain™**: The core AI logic and script/personality generation phase.
- **NexVision™**: The image and visual persona generation engine.
- **NexMotion™**: The final phase that animates the visual persona and generates lifelike video outputs with cloned or synthesized voices.

## 2. Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)
To maintain a safe environment, users of NexCHEHRA must adhere to the following content guidelines when generating personas or videos:
- **No Deepfakes of Real People**: Users are prohibited from using NexVision or NexMotion to create non-consensual deepfakes of real-world private citizens, celebrities, or public figures.
- **No Illegal or Harmful Content**: Generation of content promoting violence, hate speech, illegal activities, or explicit adult content (NSFW) is strictly forbidden.
- **Voice Cloning Restrictions**: The voice cloning feature must only be used on voices the user owns, or for which the user has explicit, legally binding permission to use.

## 3. Data Privacy & Content Ownership
- **Who owns the generated influencers?** 
  Users retain full commercial ownership and copyright of any AI influencers, images, scripts, and videos generated using NexCHEHRA, provided they do not violate the Acceptable Use Policy.
- **Data Retention**: 
  NexCHEHRA temporarily stores video generation assets (images, audio clips, and scripts) during the pipeline process securely. Final videos are stored on user dashboards but can be deleted by the user at any time. We do not use user-generated visual assets to train foundational AI models without consent.

## 4. Usage Limits and Rate Policies
Because generating high-quality video and AI imagery is resource-intensive:
- **Concurrent Generations**: Users are limited to a specific number of concurrent video generation tasks based on their subscription tier to prevent server overloading.
- **Fair Use Token Limits**: AI text generation (via NexBrain) and speech synthesis operate on a monthly token allocation. Once a user exhausts their tokens, generation speeds may be temporarily reduced, or the user may be asked to upgrade their plan.

## 5. Refund and Subscription Policy
- **Subscriptions**: NexCHEHRA operates on a SaaS (Software as a Service) monthly billing model.
- **Refunds**: Due to the immediate high costs of GPU computing required for AI generation, refunds are not automatically provided for compute time that has already been utilized. However, if a generation fails due to a system error on NexCHEHRA's end, the user's account will be credited back the generation tokens.
- **Cancellations**: Users can cancel their subscription at any time. Access to the generated history will remain available until the end of the current billing cycle.

## 6. Troubleshooting & Generation Times
- **How long does video generation take?** 
  Standard influencer generation across the NexBrain, NexVision, and NexMotion pipeline usually takes between 2 to 5 minutes, depending on the complexity of the script and platform load.
- **What to do if a generation fails?** 
  If a generation gets stuck in the NexMotion phase, users should refresh their dashboard after 5 minutes. If it officially errors out, the pipeline will automatically refund the tokens used for that specific attempt.
