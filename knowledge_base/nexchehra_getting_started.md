# NexCHEHRA Quick Start Guide: How to Create Your First Influencer

Welcome to NexCHEHRA! Generating your first AI influencer video takes just a few steps. Follow this guide to go from zero to a fully animated video in under 5 minutes.

## Prerequisites
Before you start, make sure you have:
1. A registered NexCHEHRA account (sign up at the landing page if you haven't already).
2. A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari — latest version recommended).
3. A stable internet connection (video generation requires uploading and downloading media files).

## Step 1: Design the Persona & Select a Niche (NexVision)
1. Navigate to the **Dashboard** after logging in.
2. Choose an influencer from the **"Select Influencer"** gallery to use as a base persona. We strongly recommend selecting an influencer that matches your product's niche:
   * **Aira:** Best for Beauty & Skincare (e.g., serums, face oils, cosmetics).
   * **Marcus:** Best for Fitness & Wellness (e.g., supplements, activewear).
   * **Franklin:** Best for Tech & Gadgets (e.g., earbuds, smart home devices).
   * **Liberty:** Best for American Lifestyle & Home Decor (e.g., candles, kitchen gadgets).
3. **Alternative:** You can also click "+ Custom" to upload your own **custom reference image** (JPG, PNG, or WebP, max 16MB) to build a completely new persona.
4. Enter a detailed prompt describing exactly how you want your influencer to look in the **"Scene Description / Outfit"** box. Be specific about their clothing, background, and lighting.
5. Select a **Shot Type**: Auto-Detect (AI decides), Headshot (close-up), Half Body (waist up), or Full Body (head to toe).

## Step 2: Write the Script (NexBrain)
1. You can either type your own script in the **"Video Script"** box, OR leave it blank and NexBrain will auto-generate an engaging, influencer-style script for you.
   * *Example:* Leave the description as "A lifestyle vlogger promoting a skincare product" and NexBrain will draft a 30-word Hook-Demo-Close script perfectly timed for 15 seconds.
2. Click **"🧠 ANALYZE WITH AI"** to run Phase 0 (NexBrain). The AI will generate an optimized image prompt, motion prompt, and video script.

## Step 3: Review & Edit Prompts
1. After Phase 0 completes, you'll see the AI-generated **Image Prompt**, **Motion Prompt**, and **Video Script**.
2. Edit any of these to fine-tune the output before proceeding.
3. The cost estimate is displayed so you know the price before generating.

## Step 4: Generate the Portrait (NexVision)
1. Click **"🎨 GENERATE PORTRAIT →"** to run Phase 1.
2. NexVision will generate a high-definition portrait based on your prompt and reference image.
3. Review the portrait — if you're not happy, go back and adjust the image prompt.

## Step 5: Animate & Generate Video (NexMotion)
1. Review the portrait and finalize the script.
2. Select your preferred **Audio Mode**: No Voice (silent), English, or Chinese.
3. Click **"🎬 GENERATE VIDEO →"** to run Phase 2.
4. The NexMotion engine will take 2-5 minutes to lip-sync the voice to your image and apply lifelike facial animations.
5. The final `.mp4` video will appear in the Result section, ready to download and share on social media.

## Using a Custom Reference Image
Instead of selecting a pre-made influencer from the gallery, you can upload your own face/character image:
1. Click the **"+ Custom"** card in the influencer gallery.
2. Upload a clear, well-lit photo (JPG, PNG, or WebP).
3. The AI will preserve the facial identity from your uploaded image and apply the outfit, background, and style you describe.

## Tips for First-Time Users
- **Be specific in descriptions:** Instead of "a girl in a cafe", try "A 25-year-old female influencer in a white hoodie, sitting in a brightly lit Tokyo coffee shop, sunlight through window, 8k, photorealistic."
- **Start with Half Body shots:** They work best for most use cases (product demos, talking-head videos).
- **Keep scripts around 32 words:** This is the sweet spot for 15-second videos at natural speaking pace.
- **Use the product placement feature** for brand/product ad videos — it helps the AI naturally integrate the product into the scene.
- **Check the Prompt Engineering Guide** for advanced tips on getting the best results from NexVision.
