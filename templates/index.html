FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is NEXCHEHRA? +
NEXCHEHRA is an AI-powered platform that generates realistic influencer video ads from text descriptions. It uses three proprietary AI engines — NexBrain™ for intelligent prompt analysis, NexVision™ for portrait generation, and NexMotion™ for video creation — to produce professional-quality 15-second ad content.
How long does video generation take? +
Typical generation takes 5–10 minutes. The AI pipeline processes your request through three phases: prompt analysis (~2s), portrait generation (~30-60s), and video creation (~5-8 minutes). You can track progress in real-time through the generation log.
Do I need to write a script? +
No! NexBrain™ can auto-generate scripts for you using the proven Hook-Demo-Close ad formula. Simply describe your scene and the AI handles everything — including writing a natural, engaging 15-second script tailored to your product.
Can I add my own product? +
Yes! Toggle on "Product Placement" to upload your product image. Choose from 5 placement styles (in-hand, beside, on table, background, wearing) and NexVision™ will naturally composite the product into the portrait at realistic proportions.
Can I use my own face/person? +
Absolutely! Select "Custom" in the influencer gallery and upload your own reference photo. NexVision™ preserves the exact face identity while applying the described styling, outfit, and scene changes.
Can I use the videos commercially? +
Yes! All videos generated through NEXCHEHRA are yours to use commercially. You have full rights to use, modify, and distribute the generated content for your brand, marketing campaigns, or social media channels.
